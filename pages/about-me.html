About Me

Hi! I am Rachit Gupta!

I am a Computer Science Engineer (Yeah, one of the many. But hopefully, better than most ;-) ). I am very much interested in Computer Programming and I love programming in C++.

I am an Open Source Enthusiast. I really love the prospect of my piece of code being used by millions of people. I started my Open Source journey with KDE, fixing a bug for Calligra. Then I switched to LibreOffice and I have been working with them ever since. I gotta say, the open source community is really welcoming and helpful! :-)

I have some repositories of my own on GitHub, check them out if you feel like. I also like participating in online programming competitions. Although, I am not able to enter the elite group in these competitions, but still, Participating matters more than winning. Doesn't it? ;-)

Feel free to drop me a mail at rachitgupta1792@gmail.com anytime you feel like getting in touch!
